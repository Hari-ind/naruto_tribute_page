Naruto Anime Tribute

Naruto began as a Japanese manga series, written by Masashi Kishimoto, and was adapted as an anime series in 2002. The series follows the adventures of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja of the Hidden Leaf Village, whose dream is to gain the respect and recognition of his peers and to become the village Hokage, the most powerful ninja who leads the village.

Naruto is the best anime of all time and best series in the world because it is filled with a lot of life lessons that are very inspiring to all classes of audience

1. Naruto: 220 episodes
